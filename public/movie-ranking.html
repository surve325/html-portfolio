The Best Movies According to Mahesh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

RRR

This is my favourite action movie. It is an epic action drama that feels like a “party”.

Bahubali

"Bahubali" is an epic Indian movie directed by S.S. Rajamouli, which was released in two parts, "Bahubali: The Beginning" in 2015, and "Bahubali: The Conclusion" in 2017.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
